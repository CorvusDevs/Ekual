# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 30, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 30, 2026

CorvusDevs ("we", "us", "our") built Ekual ("the App") as a commercial macOS application. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects and how it is handled.

## Summary

**Ekual does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.** All audio processing happens locally on your Mac.

## Data We Do Not Collect

- No audio recordings or audio data leave your device
- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No crash reports sent to us
- No personal information (name, email, IP address, device identifiers)
- No cookies or tracking technologies
- No third-party analytics SDKs

## Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores your preferences (equalizer settings, preset selections, excluded apps, keyboard shortcut) using macOS UserDefaults. This data:

- Stays entirely on your Mac
- Is never transmitted to any server
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the App

## Audio Processing

Ekual processes system audio in real-time to apply loudness equalization. All audio processing occurs entirely on your device using Apple's Core Audio framework. No audio data is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

## License Validation

If you purchased a license, the App may communicate with Paddle (our payment processor) to validate your license key. This communication is handled by Paddle's SDK and is subject to [Paddle's Privacy Policy](https://www.paddle.com/legal/privacy). We do not receive or store any payment information.

## Third-Party Services

- **Paddle** (paddle.com) — processes payments and license validation. See [Paddle's Privacy Policy](https://www.paddle.com/legal/privacy)

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last Updated" date.
